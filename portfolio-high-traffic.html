  1. Home

Overview

This project focuses on predicting the best recipes based on customer food demand to optimize the selection of recipes to be displayed on a website's main page. By analyzing historical data, the goal is to enhance user engagement and drive traffic through well-informed recipe recommendations.

Key Features
  • Data Processing and Analysis
  • Model Selection and Building
  • Model Experimentation and Hyperparameter Tuning: Logistic Regression, Support Vector Machine Classifier, K-Nearest Neighbors, Ridge Classifier, Simple Multi-Layer Perceptron
  • Predictions and Business Insights
